Purpose

XPERYN is designed to support learning and revision. It is a study tool — not a shortcut. The goal is to help you understand engineering concepts, not to provide answers for you to copy and submit.

Your Responsibility

You are responsible for following your university's academic integrity policies. Using AI-generated content in assessed work without proper disclosure may constitute academic misconduct at many institutions.

What XPERYN Is For

  • Understanding concepts you find difficult
  • Getting step-by-step explanations of engineering problems
  • Debugging code and hardware projects
  • Revising for exams with worked examples
  • Planning project methodology and structure

What XPERYN Is Not For

  • Generating assignment answers to submit as your own work
  • Completing assessed coursework or exams on your behalf
  • Circumventing academic integrity rules at your institution

Our Position

We believe AI tools can genuinely help students learn better — when used responsibly. We encourage all users to treat XPERYN as a tutor, not a ghostwriter. If in doubt about whether your use of AI is permitted, check with your instructor or university.
