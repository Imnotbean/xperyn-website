Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address. When you use XPERYN, your messages and conversations are stored to provide chat history and improve the product experience.

How Your Data Is Used

Your conversations are used to provide the tutoring service and maintain your chat history. We may review anonymized usage patterns to improve the product. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

AI Processing

Your messages are sent to third-party AI API providers to generate responses. These providers may process your messages according to their own data policies. We recommend not including highly sensitive personal information in your conversations.

Data Storage

Your account data and conversations are stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@xperyn.my.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@xperyn.my.
